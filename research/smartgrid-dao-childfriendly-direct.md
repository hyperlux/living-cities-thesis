## DAO Governance 2025

**Core Question:**
- "Can Decentralized Governance Really Work?" (LCX Sept 2025)

**Voting Design:**
- On-chain voting: recorded as blockchain transaction
- Off-chain voting: third-party platforms
- "Plausibly most crucial governance design choice"

**Research Findings:**

**Harvard Business School (Oct 2025):**
- "May not democratize shareholder voting as much as regulators hoped"
- Reality vs. promise examined

**Frontiers in Blockchain (2025):**
- Delegated voting scoping review (8 citations)
- Multi-tiered governance structures
- Decision-support mechanisms
- "More inclusive and context-aware" governance needed

**ACM Study (Nov 2025):**
- "Novel organizational structure"
- "Decentralized, transparent governance"
- Voting power and participation issues
- Controversies documented

**Working Examples:**
- ENS DAO (Ethereum Name Service)
- Decentralized domain ownership

**Implications:** DAOs offer tools but require careful design to avoid power concentration.

## Child-Friendly Cities 2025-2026

- "Put children at the centre of urban design"
- "Public spaces essential to children's health, development, well-being"
- Response to shrinking safe play spaces in growing cities

- Network in 40+ countries
- Municipal governments, civil society, private sector, academia, media, children
- "Practical governance framework"
- "Help communities take action for and with children"

**Clean Cities Campaign Ranking 2025:**
- "Streets For Kids, Cities For All"

**15-Minute City Connection (OECD Aug 2025):**
- "Planning for Play: Designing Urban Space to Welcome Back Families"
- Schools, parks, daily needs within short walk/ride
- Play-centered design supports broader sustainability vision

**Core Principle:**
- "Safe streets and parks, supportive communities"
