## Synthesis: Local Economy + Public Space + Anchors for Living Cities

| Domain | Current | Living City |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Retail | Chain dominance | Main street local |
| Public Space | Car-centric | Pedestrian plazas |
| Procurement | Global supply chains | Anchor institution local |

**Living City Design:**

1. **Main Street Economy:**
   - Local ownership prioritized
   - Chain stores limited or excluded
   - Business models rooted in place
   - Community integration required

2. **Public Space Network:**
   - Pedestrian-only plazas
   - "Spark joy" design principles
   - All ages, inclusive
   - Biophilic elements throughout

3. **Community Wealth Building:**
   - Anchor institutions (schools, clinics) buy local
   - Worker cooperative preference
   - Workforce from community
   - Investment stays local

**CWB Components:**
- Procurement: Local first
- Workforce: Hire local
- Investment: Reinvest locally
- Land: Community ownership

**Metric:**
- % of retail locally owned
- m² of pedestrian space per resident
- % of anchor procurement local
