## Synthesis: Governance + Tools + Investment for Living Cities

| Domain | Status Quo | Living City |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Governance | Representative only | Citizen assemblies |
| Planning | Expert-driven | AI-assisted, human-centered |
| Housing | Market-dependent | Publicly funded |

**Living City Design:**

1. **Citizen Assemblies:**
   - Neighborhood assemblies for local decisions
   - Binding recommendations
   - Collaborative governance
   - Trust-building through participation

2. **AI Planning Tools:**
   - Efficiency gains
   - Better living conditions
   - Human-in-the-loop always
   - NOT surveillance
   - Open source tools preferred

3. **Public Housing Investment:**
   - Direct public funding
   - Long-term commitment (10+ years)
   - Build, rehabilitate, operate
   - Remove market dependency

**Metric:**
- % of decisions through citizen assembly
- AI tool efficiency gains
- % of housing publicly funded
