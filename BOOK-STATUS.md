## Key Findings from Research Swarm

### What's Working (2025-2026)
- **Culdesac Tempe**: First US car-free neighborhood, actually operational
- **Toyota Woven City**: Honest R&D lab, under construction
- **Decidim**: 11,500+ participatory processes globally
- **CLT expansion**: Now in Brazil, Mexico, Belgium, Germany, Netherlands
- **Mozilla Democracy x AI**: Active incubator for civic AI tools

### What's Failing
- **NEOM**: Catastrophic. The Line scaled from 170km to 2.4km, $8.8T overruns, CEO fired
- **Telosa**: 4+ years, still no site, vaporware trajectory
- **Pr√≥spera**: $10.7B lawsuit from Honduras, legal existential crisis
- **Porto Alegre**: Original participatory budgeting city *suspended* PB

### Critical Gaps Identified
1. **Production problem**: What do living cities *make*?
2. **Legitimacy problem**: No theory for long-term state recognition
3. **Reproduction problem**: All projects are demographic sinks
4. **Megacity scale**: Examples skew toward smaller cities
5. **Transition economics**: How to fund the 5-10 year gap?
